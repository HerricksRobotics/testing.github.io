Board

All members of the Herricks Robotics Club Board are passionate and determined individuals who will go to any extreme for just robotics.

President: Caitlin Chou

Caitlin Chou, a senior, and the leader of 11040B. She is possibly the most experienced out of all the members in robotics. She has been competing in VEX competitions since she was in 8th Grade, when she went to Worlds. She is currently part of two robotics teams, 11040B, and her church team.

Vice President: Herb Hagemann

Herb Hagemann, a senior, and a member of 11040A. He is possibly the most resourceful board member out of all the members in robotics. His outgoing personality makes him a wonderful Vice President

Director of Engineering: Richard Tsai

Richard Tsai, a senior, and the team leader of 11040A. His knowledge in everything related to Robotics makes him the first stop for all Robotics members. From coding to website designing to engineering a robot, he knows it all!

Public Relations Officer: Bhavesh Patel

Bhavesh Patel, a junior, a member of 11040A and the Public Relations Officer. He organizes fundraising events, and competitions. Without him our club would most likely not have the proper funding neccessary to run the club.

Director of Programming: Christopher Lo
